About Limestione

Limestone is a project management tool that provides multiple functionalities to help guarantee a safer path for your project' s workflow with: A topics management system where you can easily divide tasks and plan meetings according to precise topics to avoid wasting time and ressources Time-tracking for each task and mission for more accurate data and better future prediction. Document and outcomes management to save and arrange data to help organize work , increase efficiency and thus, assure well-defined, clear and achievable goals
